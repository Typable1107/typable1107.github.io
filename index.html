About Me

image

Hey, I'm Typable. I grew up in Washington and have been making music since 2019. I play piano, guitar, trombone, and tuba, and I write everything myself.

My music pulls influence from many electronic artists, like Andrew Huang, Jeremy Blake, and Vylet Pony. While I do idolize these people, I still care a lot about individuality and uniqueness.

Outside of music I'm into programming and digital 3d art. If you want to reach out about anything, the contact section is down below.
